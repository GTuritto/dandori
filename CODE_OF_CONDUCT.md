# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Dandori is a framework built on the belief that preparation and precision improve outcomes. We extend that principle to our community: thoughtful, precise communication leads to better collaboration.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Share real-world experience and data to support proposals
- Engage with ideas constructively, even when disagreeing
- Respect that different team contexts require different adaptations
- Credit prior work and acknowledge contributions

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Personal attacks or dismissive language toward contributors
- Promoting commercial products or services disguised as framework contributions
- Publishing others' experience reports without their consent

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Dandori community spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any associated communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed.
